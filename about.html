About me

I'm Tania Caldera. I was born in Venezuela but I've been living in Ireland since 2018. My passion for baking started very early. My parents own a bakery in the small town where I grew (they still do!). My products are not only full of quality and delicious ingredients, but also with love. They are piece of family, a piece of home. And I hope you can feel that in every sweet (and savoury) bite!
